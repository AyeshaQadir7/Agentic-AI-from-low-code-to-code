This repository contains my research assignment for the **Agentic AI Use Case** assignment.  
The goal of the assignment was to find **one real company using agentic AI**, research how they use it, and explain it in simple terms.

## Selected Company: **Bank of America**

### üß† AI Technology Used
Bank of America uses two main AI systems:

**Erica (Customer-facing AI assistant):** Helps customers with banking tasks, financial insights & automated support

**Agentic AI for Internal Workflows:** Automates compliance checks, fraud alerts, document processing & employee support tasks

---

## üìç Key Findings

- Bank of America faced large operational loads from customer service, fraud monitoring, and compliance tasks  
- AI agents automate high-volume, rule-based tasks  
- **90%+ employees now use AI tools** daily  
- Erica has processed **2+ billion customer interactions**  
- Agents reduce manual workload, improve productivity, and accelerate decision-making  
- Humans remain in control by reviewing critical actions and supervising AI workflows
